9. Ads, Tracking, and Privacy Preferences

The application may display advertisements. Rewarded ads are shown only as a result of an action selected by the user. Advertising providers may process limited data for ad impressions, clicks, measurement, fraud prevention, and advertising preferences.

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, and similar regions, a consent screen may be displayed where required for advertising and personalization. On iOS, the application may display Apple’s permission prompt for advertising or measurement activities that require tracking permission. If the user does not grant permission, some ads may be non-personalized or certain advertising/measurement features may be limited.

10. Notifications

If the user grants permission, the application may send low-stock alerts, reminders, in-app status, backup, subscription, or important service notifications. Marketing notifications may require explicit consent under applicable law. The user can disable notifications from the device settings.

11. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Purchase and subscription transactions are handled by Google Play, Apple App Store, or the relevant application store. Devovia does not view full payment card information. Purchase status, subscription status, transaction ID, and entitlement information are processed only to provide access to purchased features, verify purchases, manage refunds/cancellations, and prevent abuse.

Subscription cancellation, renewal, pricing, trial periods, refunds, and billing processes are subject to the rules of the relevant application store. The user can manage their subscription through their application store account.

12. International Data Transfers

Devovia may be based in the Netherlands; however, service providers may process data outside the user’s country. These transfers may be carried out under applicable data protection laws, contractual commitments, standard contractual clauses, adequacy decisions, data processing agreements, or similar protection mechanisms.

13. Retention Periods

Data is retained for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it is processed:

  • local inventory data may remain on the device until the user deletes it, uninstalls the application, or clears device data;
  • cloud backups may be retained for as long as the user account is active, until the user deletes them, or until the backup service is terminated;
  • account and contract acceptance records may be retained for as long as necessary for account management and legal proof purposes;
  • purchase, subscription, billing, and security records may be retained for the duration required by legal obligations and dispute limitation periods;
  • support correspondence may be retained for a reasonable period for request resolution, quality, and legal requirements;
  • advertising and measurement data may be retained according to providers’ policies and the user’s consent/preference status;
  • after deletion, backup copies may remain in system backups for a limited period and are deleted or anonymized within the ordinary backup cycle.

14. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

The user may initiate an account deletion request from the account management section within the application. Under applicable store rules, applications that support account creation must also provide a way to request account deletion outside the application. Therefore, users may also contact info@devoviastudio.com for account and data deletion requests.

When a deletion request is received, personal data and cloud data associated with the account will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period. However, certain data may be retained to the extent necessary for legal obligations, payment records, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, accounting, or the exercise of legal rights.

Since local device data is stored on the user’s device, the user must use in-app deletion tools, delete exported files, or clear the application data from device settings.
