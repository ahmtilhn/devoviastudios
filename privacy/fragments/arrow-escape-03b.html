14. Security

Arrow Escape uses platform-provided purchase, Game Center, Google Play Games, consent, and advertising SDKs rather than collecting payment credentials itself. Local game progress is stored within the app’s device storage.

No digital system is completely risk-free. Users should keep their device and platform account secure and should not share purchase or account credentials.

15. Choices and Consent

  • Tracking permission can be allowed or denied through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt and changed later in device Settings.
  • Advertising-consent choices may be presented through Google User Messaging Platform where required.
  • Personalized-ad choices can also be managed through device and platform advertising settings.
  • Optional rewarded advertisements are initiated by the user.
  • Game Center and Google Play Games availability depends on the user’s platform sign-in and settings.
  • The app remains playable when tracking permission is declined or platform gaming services are unavailable.

16. User Rights

Depending on applicable law, users may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or information about processing controlled by Devovia. Users can delete local game data by removing the app or clearing its app data.
