15. User Rights

Depending on your country, you may have the following rights:

  • to access your personal data;
  • to request correction of your data;
  • to request deletion or anonymization of your data;
  • to restrict or object to processing;
  • to withdraw your consent;
  • to request data portability;
  • to manage your advertising, targeted advertising, or data sharing preferences;
  • to object to the sale or sharing of personal data;
  • to limit the use of sensitive personal data;
  • to request information about automated decision-making;
  • to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority.

15.1 European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland

Users in these regions may have the rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, data portability, withdrawal of consent, and complaint to a supervisory authority.

15.2 Türkiye

Users in Türkiye may have the right to learn whether their personal data is being processed, request information if it has been processed, learn the purpose of processing, know the third parties to whom data has been transferred, request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data, request deletion or destruction, request notification of correction/deletion operations to third parties to whom the data has been transferred, object to results arising from automated analysis, and claim compensation if they suffer damage.
