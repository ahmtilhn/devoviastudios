19. Advertisements and External Destinations

Arrow Escape may display advertisements supplied through Google Mobile Ads and configured mediation partners. Ad providers control the creative content and destination shown after an ad is selected.

Ads may open an external website, an app-store page, or another third-party service. Devovia does not control the privacy practices of those destinations. Users should review the destination’s terms and privacy policy before providing information.

The app does not require a user to click an advertisement to access ordinary gameplay. Rewarded ads are optional and grant the reward described before the ad begins.

20. Internet Connection and Offline Use

Core puzzle gameplay and local progress can generally operate without a Devovia account. An internet connection may be required for advertisements, rewarded-ad verification, purchases and restoration, Game Center or Google Play Games authentication, leaderboards, achievements, Android saved-game synchronization, consent updates, and external legal or support pages.

If a network or third-party service is unavailable, the related online feature may be delayed or unavailable while local gameplay continues where technically possible.
