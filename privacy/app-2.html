Feature-linked privacy areas

The app should request access only when a feature needs it, with platform controls remaining available to the user.

Location and direction

Location may be used to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. These experiences are designed around a clear user action.

  • Prayer-time calculation
  • Qibla direction
  • Location permission controlled by the operating system

Notifications

Reminder schedules help users follow prayer times and spiritual routines when notification permission is enabled.

  • Prayer alerts
  • Daily reminder preferences
  • Local scheduling and permission-aware behavior

Local preferences

Settings that personalize the experience may be stored locally so the app can restore the user's chosen routine.

  • Language and theme
  • Bookmarks and saved content
  • Notification and premium-state cache

Advertising and purchases

Where ads or premium purchases are available, eligible providers may process information under their own terms.

  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data
  • Google Play or App Store purchase processing
  • No direct collection of payment-card details by Devovia

Your controls

Permissions can be changed later, although disabling them can limit the feature they support.

Permission choices

Location and notification access can be enabled, reviewed or removed through device settings. Core reading features can remain available even when optional permissions are not granted.

Questions and requests

Privacy questions, data requests or account-related concerns can be routed through Devovia support and the procedure described in the official policy.
