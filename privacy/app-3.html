The text below is the full TinySteps privacy policy provided by the developer.

Overview

TinySteps is a calm, local-first habit and routine tracker. We do not require account creation. Your habit data is stored on your device. We do not sell personal data.

Who we are

This app is developed and published by TinySteps (Ahmet Ilhan), based in the Netherlands.

Email: ilhanahmet246@gmail.com
Phone: +31 06 14358015

Data we collect

We collect only what is needed to make the app work and to provide optional monetization features.

Data stored locally on your device

  • Habit name and schedule
  • Reminder settings
  • Habit completion logs
  • Theme and settings preferences

This data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to any server.

Ads (Google AdMob)

Free users may see ads. Google AdMob may collect advertising ID, device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data.

Purchases (Google Play Billing)

Purchases are handled by Google Play. We receive purchase status only.

Notifications

If enabled, notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

What we do NOT do

  • No account required
  • No server-side habit storage
  • No selling of personal data
  • No cross-app tracking

Data retention

Data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Children’s privacy

TinySteps is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy with a new effective date.
