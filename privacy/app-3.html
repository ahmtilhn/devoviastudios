How privacy connects to the habit loop

The app keeps the core workflow simple while making optional platform services understandable.

Habit records

The information users create to follow their routine may be stored locally on the device.

  • Habit names and schedules
  • Completion logs and streak history
  • Routine, theme and display preferences

Reminders

When notifications are enabled, the app can schedule local reminders to keep habits visible at the chosen time.

  • User-selected reminder times
  • Local notification scheduling
  • Permission controlled through the operating system

Advertising

Free users may see advertising provided through eligible third-party services such as Google AdMob.

  • Advertising identifiers where permitted
  • Ad interaction and device context
  • Provider processing under its own terms

Premium purchases

Optional premium purchases are processed through the platform store rather than directly by Devovia.

  • Google Play Billing processing
  • No direct payment-card collection by Devovia
  • Local or store-linked premium state

Your choices, retention and support

The product is designed so users can understand which platform capability supports each feature.

Notifications and device data

Notification access can be changed through device settings. Removing access stops reminder delivery but does not prevent basic habit tracking.

Retention and deletion

Habit records and preferences stored locally remain on the device until the user edits, clears or removes them through available app or device controls.

Questions and requests

Privacy questions, data requests and billing concerns can be routed through Devovia support.
