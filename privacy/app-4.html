How data connects to gameplay

The core puzzle can be understood separately from optional advertising and purchase services.

Game progress

Progress information may remain locally on the device so the game can restore the player's current state.

  • Level and board progression
  • Coins, stars and boosters
  • Missions, streaks and game settings

Advertising

Google AdMob may process information required to deliver, measure and protect advertising experiences where ads are shown.

  • Advertising identifiers where permitted
  • Device and approximate location context
  • Ad views, clicks and rewarded-ad interactions

Optional purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and are subject to Google Play's purchase and payment terms.

  • Store-managed purchase processing
  • No direct payment-card collection by Devovia
  • Purchase state used to unlock eligible content

Diagnostics

Technical data may be used to understand crashes, compatibility and game performance where supported by enabled services.

  • App and device version context
  • Crash or performance signals
  • Gameplay events required to support rewards or progression

Your controls

Advertising, purchases and device-level privacy options remain connected to Google Play and Android settings.

Advertising and platform settings

Eligible advertising controls, purchase history and account-level settings are managed through Google and the device platform. Available choices can vary by age, region and operating-system version.

Questions and support

Privacy, purchase or gameplay questions can be sent through Devovia support. The official policy and current Google Play Data Safety section should be used for the complete disclosure.
