Privacy Policy — Arrow Escape

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Arrow Escape, published by Devovia.

This policy explains how the app handles user data, what is stored locally on the device, which third-party services are used, and what rights users have.

1. Publisher and Developer Information

Publisher: Devovia
Developer: Ahmet İlhan
Contact email: ilhan.ahmet246@gmail.com

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, data usage, advertising, or user rights, you can contact us at the email address above.

2. About the App

Arrow Escape is a mobile puzzle game where players follow arrow directions, manage time, use memory, and complete challenging levels.

The app does not include user login, online accounts, membership registration, or cloud profiles.

Game progress and some app settings are stored locally on the user’s device. These local records are not sent to Devovia servers.

3. No User Account or Login System

Arrow Escape does not allow or require users to create an account.

The app does not collect or request:

  • Name or surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Username
  • Password
  • Profile photo
  • Social media account
  • Personal profile information
  • Identity information

Users can play the game without registering, logging in, or providing personal account information.

4. Locally Stored Data

Arrow Escape may store certain gameplay data locally on the user’s device in order to provide a smooth game experience.

This local data may include:

  • Current level
  • Completed levels
  • Coins
  • Stars
  • Booster counts
  • Daily mission progress
  • Streak reward progress
  • Game settings
  • Sound and music preferences
  • Local gameplay progress

This information is stored only on the device.

Devovia does not upload this local gameplay data to its own servers. The data is not linked to an online user account because the app does not have an account system.

If the user deletes the app, clears app data, changes device, or resets local progress, this local data may be lost.

5. Online Services

Arrow Escape does not use online accounts, cloud save, chat systems, user profiles, social login, or Devovia-owned servers.

The only online third-party service used in the app is Google AdMob for advertising.

6. Advertising and Google AdMob

Arrow Escape may display ads through Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect or process certain data in order to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, improve ad delivery, and support personalized or non-personalized advertising.

Google AdMob may process information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Ad impressions
  • Ad interactions
  • Diagnostic information
  • App activity related to ads
  • Technical data required to deliver advertisements

This data is processed by Google and its advertising services, not directly by Devovia.

Users can manage personalized advertising preferences through their device settings or Google advertising settings.

7. Personalized and Non-Personalized Ads

Depending on the user’s region, device settings, consent status, and Google’s advertising systems, the app may show personalized or non-personalized ads.

Personalized ads may be based on advertising identifiers, app activity, general location, and other signals handled by Google.

Non-personalized ads may still use limited data such as device information, IP-based approximate location, and basic ad delivery data.

8. No Sensitive Personal Data Collection

Arrow Escape does not directly collect sensitive personal data.

The app does not request or collect:

  • Health information
  • Financial information
  • Government ID
  • Exact location
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Microphone recordings
  • Camera data
  • Biometric information
  • Religious, political, or ethnic information

The app is designed as a local-first puzzle game without account-based personal data collection.

9. Children’s Privacy

Arrow Escape does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The app does not include account creation, user profiles, chat, direct messaging, or personal information forms.

However, the app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Parents or legal guardians are encouraged to review device settings, ad settings, and parental controls when children use the app.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through any unexpected method, please contact us at:

ilhan.ahmet246@gmail.com

10. Purpose of Data Use

Local gameplay data is used only to provide and improve the game experience.

This includes:

  • Saving progress
  • Continuing from the current level
  • Keeping coin and reward status
  • Saving booster counts
  • Remembering sound and music settings
  • Tracking daily missions locally
  • Managing streak rewards locally
  • Improving gameplay continuity

Google AdMob data is used by Google for advertising, ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and ad performance purposes.

11. Data Sharing

Devovia does not sell, rent, or trade users’ personal information.

Since Arrow Escape does not have a login system and does not collect direct account information, Devovia does not share user account data with third parties.

However, Google AdMob may process certain advertising and device-related data as described in Google’s own policies.

12. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on the user’s device as long as the app remains installed and the local app data is not deleted.

Users can delete local data by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Clearing app data from device settings
  • Using a reset progress feature, if available in the app
  • Resetting or changing their device

Advertising-related data processed by Google AdMob is subject to Google’s own data retention policies.

13. Data Security

Arrow Escape stores gameplay progress locally on the user’s device.

Devovia aims to keep the app stable and secure, but no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely risk-free.

Because local game data is stored on the device, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own device. If the device is lost, reset, damaged, or cleared, local game progress may be lost.

  • Understand how their data is handled

  • Contact the developer with privacy questions

  • Object to inaccurate or unclear privacy information
  • Exercise applicable legal rights under relevant privacy laws

Devovia is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party advertisers, websites, or services.

Users should review the privacy policies of any third-party services they choose to visit.

18. Internet Connection

Arrow Escape can store game progress locally on the device.

However, an internet connection may be required for:

  • Watching rewarded ads
  • Receiving ad-based rewards

If there is no internet connection, some ad-based features may not be available.

If in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Any future payment processing through Google Play would be handled by Google Play. Devovia would not collect or store users’ payment card details.
